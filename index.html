Our Review Architecture

01 / Application Submission

Submit your profile alongside comprehensive documentation of architectural case studies or clear repository components.

We review layout mechanics, documentation architecture, and your approach to addressing layout optimization layers.

02 / Technical Resume Review

Engineering leads parse code formats, theme customization workflows, or quantitative marketing sheets directly.

No generic screening filters. Real execution specialists map your background directly to active client assignments.

03 / Asynchronous HR Discussion

A brief operational dialogue covering compensation, geographic layout setup, and collaborative culture matching.

We discuss your optimal workflow patterns, communication preferences, and focus habits.

04 / Practical Assessment Round

A focused technical verification problem based on real agency projects—no abstract algorithms.

You'll analyze architecture limits, design custom Liquid setups, or optimize specific script structures.

05 / Final System Alignment

Review engineering guidelines with our design leads and primary brand engineers.

We finalize your role alignment, project ownership scopes, and primary tools integration.

06 / Active Offer 🎉

A structured proposal detailing hardware support, custom options, and your onboarding sequence.

We provision your gear, clear secure accounts, and integrate you cleanly into the team flow.
